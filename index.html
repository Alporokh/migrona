Services

Our services

We provide a full range of freight forwarding services for cargo delivery by road, and arrange transport of complete, groupage, oversized, dangerous, general, small, chemical, fragile, valuable and long cargo.

01

International transport

International freight transport of export-import and transit cargo by road.

02

Groupage & oversized

Delivery of small-batch and groupage cargo. Transport of oversized and heavy cargo.

03

Vehicle transport

Transport of passenger cars by car carriers.

04

Customs & documents

Consolidation of cargo at warehouses in Europe; control and processing of accompanying documents; declaration and customs clearance of cargo.

05

Insurance & warehousing

Functions related to cargo insurance and security, warehousing and packaging, receiving cargo on consignment, and obtaining necessary certificates.

06

Consulting

Consulting on international freight transport.
